## What we collect
**In the current version, the app does not collect, transmit, or share any
personal data.** Your learning progress (XP, streaks, completed lessons, settings)
is stored **only on your device**. We do not use analytics, advertising, or
third-party trackers, and we have no servers that receive your data.

## Prompts you write
Lesson exercises let you write prompts that are evaluated locally on your device.
In this version, prompt text is **not sent anywhere**.

> When live AI grading is enabled in a future version, the prompts you submit will
> be sent to our grading service / model provider to return feedback. This policy
> will be updated to disclose that processing, the provider(s), and retention
> before that feature ships.

## Permissions
The app requests no sensitive permissions to run. If you enable **reminders** in a
future version, the app will request notification permission solely to deliver the
daily reminder you configured; you can revoke it in system settings at any time.

## Children's privacy
The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data
from them.

## Data retention & deletion
Because data lives only on your device, uninstalling the app removes it. There is
nothing stored on our side to delete.
